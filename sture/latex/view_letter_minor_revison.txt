Reviewer #1: Summary:
This paper proposes a new approach for solving the data association problem encountered in the setting of online frame-by-frame tracking-by-detection MOT. The STURE method is a combination of three training losses for learning a joint neural embedding space for sequence and detection features. Ablation studies demonstrate that adopting this training scheme improves tracking performance

Updated review:
Thank you for addressing the reviewer comments in the revised manuscript. I have the following minor questions:

-Something that is not apparent to me is how the detection and compressed sequence features are combined to produce the reinforced feature. Are they just concatenated? (The input to the linear classifier in Fig. 2 is 4,096 dimensional?). Please update the paper to highlight this step.

-Does Fig 5a and 5b show the (4,096?) dimensional "reinforced" feature? If so, this should be specified.

-Are public detections used for all MOT experiments? Whether each tracker uses public or private detections should be indicated in each results table. Trackers should only be compared if they are using the same source of object detections.

I also encourage another pass of revising the grammar of the manuscript. There are many sentences still with improper English grammar.



审稿人 #1：总结：
本文提出了一种解决在线逐帧跟踪检测MOT设置中遇到的数据关联问题的新方法。 STURE 方法是三种训练损失的组合，用于学习用于序列和检测特征的联合神经嵌入空间。消融研究表明，采用这种训练方案可以提高跟踪性能

更新评论：
感谢您在修订稿中处理审稿人的意见。我有以下小问题：

- 对我来说并不明显的是检测和压缩序列特征如何组合以产生增强特征。它们只是串联吗？ （图 2 中线性分类器的输入是 4,096 维？）。请更新论文以突出显示此步骤。

- 图 5a 和 5b 是否显示了（4,096？）尺寸“强化”特征？如果是这样，则应指定。

-所有MOT实验都使用公共检测吗？每个跟踪器是否使用公共或私人检测应在每个结果表中说明。仅当使用相同的对象检测源时，才应比较跟踪器。

我还鼓励再次修改手稿的语法。仍有许多句子的英语语法不正确。
